About Borderless Studio

We're a small, focused team bridging Dutch and Polish markets through smart web design.

Our story

Borderless Studio was born from a simple observation: Polish entrepreneurs in the Netherlands struggle to find web designers who understand both cultures. Dutch agencies don't get the Polish market. Polish freelancers don't understand Dutch business expectations. We're the bridge — a team that speaks both languages (literally and culturally) and builds websites that work across borders.

How we work

No bloated processes. No account managers you'll never meet. You talk directly to the people building your site. We scope together, design fast, deliver in days. Every project gets a dedicated timeline and transparent pricing — you always know what's happening and when.

What we stand for

  • Speed without compromise
  • Transparent pricing — no surprises
  • Direct communication with builders
  • Cross-border thinking by default
  • Your success is our portfolio
